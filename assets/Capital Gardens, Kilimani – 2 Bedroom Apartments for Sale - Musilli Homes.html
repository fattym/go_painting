Modern Living | Prime Kilimani Location | Elegant Design | Affordable Luxury

Discover Capital Gardens Kilimani, an exceptional residential development located in one of Nairobi’s most vibrant and desirable neighborhoods. Nestled in the heart of Kilimani, this stunning gated community features high-end 1, 2, and 3-bedroom apartments, including spacious 3-bedroom units with DSQ, all meticulously designed for urban comfort, security, and luxury living.

🛏️ 2 Bedroom Apartment for Sale in Kilimani – From KSh 10.55M

Perfect for young professionals, couples, or small families, the 2-bedroom apartments at Capital Gardens Kilimani offer a refined balance of functionality and elegance. With two layout options available – 96 SQM from KSh 10.55M and a larger 102 SQM unit at KSh 11M (all ensuite) – these homes are tailored for modern urban lifestyles.

Key Features:

  • ✅ Spacious 2 Bedrooms – generous room sizes with contemporary finishes
  • ✅ All Ensuite (102 SQM option) – ensuring privacy and comfort
  • ✅ Closed Kitchen Plan – sleek and functional layout with quality finishes
  • ✅ Dedicated Wash Area – adds convenience and practicality
  • ✅ Modern Living & Dining Area – perfect for entertaining and relaxing
  • ✅ Ample Natural Lighting – large windows and high ceilings

With premium interior finishes and thoughtful design, this 2-bedroom apartment in Kilimani is ideal for both home ownership and rental investment.

Size: 173 SQM | 2 Ensuite Bedrooms | Servant Quarter | Balcony

Key Highlights:

  • ✅ 3 Large Bedrooms – 2 are ensuite for added privacy
  • ✅ DSQ with Separate Access – perfect for staff or guest use
  • ✅ Closed Kitchen Plan – with ample storage and quality fittings
  • ✅ Pantry and Wash Area – extra utility for daily convenience
  • ✅ Spacious Living Room – opens to a private balcony in the master bedroom
  • ✅ Modern Bathrooms – high-quality fittings and stylish finishes

This 3-bedroom apartment in Kilimani with a DSQ offers more than just space – it delivers a high standard of living in a central, well-connected location.

🏙️ Prime Location – Why Invest in Kilimani?

Kilimani is one of Nairobi’s fastest-growing residential and commercial hubs. Living at Capital Gardens means you’re minutes away from:

  • Shopping malls like Yaya Centre, Prestige Plaza, and Adlife Plaza
  • Top-tier hospitals including Nairobi Women’s Hospital and The Nairobi Hospital
  • Excellent schools like Makini School, Riara Group, and French School
  • An array of restaurants, cafés, and essential services

Whether you’re buying for your own use or as an investment, Capital Gardens Kilimani is a smart, future-forward choice with strong rental demand and capital appreciation.
